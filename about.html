Dreacone is a high-quality polypropylene sheet manufactured by Polypro Industries. It is a lightweight, rigid and durable sheet material designed for applications such as packaging, printing, signage, partitions, construction formwork and various industrial uses.
Dreacone sheets are available in a variety of thicknesses and standard sheet sizes. Customized dimensions may also be possible, subject to minimum order quantities. Please contact our sales team with your required thickness, sheet size and quantity for detailed options.
Yes. Dreacone polypropylene sheets can be die-cut, creased, welded, riveted and fabricated using standard equipment. The surface is suitable for various printing methods, making it ideal for printed displays, signage and branded packaging.
Certain Dreacone grades are suitable for outdoor use, offering good resistance to moisture and many environmental conditions. For long-term outdoor exposure or UV-intensive environments, please share your specific requirements so we can suggest the most appropriate grade.
Dreacone is made from polypropylene (PP), which is recyclable where local facilities exist. We encourage customers to collect and send used material into appropriate recycling streams in line with regional regulations and recycling capabilities.
